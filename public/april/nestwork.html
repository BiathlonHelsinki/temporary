Nestwork
Spring 2017

As wavering phenomena become frozen through frantic and critical practice, the viewer is left with a hymn to the edges of our condition.

And when these practices unravel - what remains of the self?

Can a collective energy flicker through the simulacra?

Temporary has launched the Nestwork project, a series of seminars and process-workshops investigating the essential, critical praxis that remains after all else has been distilled.

Taking on experimental formats, and scheduled rhizomatically, Nestwork aims to attenuate the fear of speech, proclaim the impossibility of articulation, and catalogue forgotten connotations.
