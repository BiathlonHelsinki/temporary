ArtWorks in Progress
March/April 2017

How can art challenge the dominant methodologies of the post-Fordist state? How does research play a role in questioning these pressures, and how are today's cutting-edge artists pushing against these boundaries?

Hy Grynszpan, curator, critic and art historian

ArtWorks In Progress seeks to drive open the space between start and finish, unfolding the seedy underbelly of artistic research and eviscerating the distinctions of procedures.

The works in ArtWorks in Progress will escape frmo the quotidian expectations of the gallery, the space, and the cube; what will remain is undeniably bold and counter-factually evident.
