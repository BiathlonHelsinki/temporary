ABOUT

Temporary Art Space focuses on the production of contemporary art that reflects upon the context of this milieu while investigating other commonalities.

Temporary is poised upon the precipe of the precarious, probing aesthetic sensations against the onrush of the sublime incentive. Where subterfuge becomes not just a coping mechanism but the primary procedural truth, Temporary will become an avalanche that defies the ineluctable and castrates the sublime.
